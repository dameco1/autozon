# Go-to-Market Strategy

## Executive Summary

Autozon's go-to-market strategy follows a **concentrated launch** approach: dominate one market (Austria) before expanding. Austria serves as the ideal proving ground — small enough to achieve meaningful market share quickly, large enough to validate unit economics, and culturally/linguistically aligned with the primary expansion target (Germany).

---

## Market Entry: Austria First

### Why Austria

| Factor | Advantage |
|---|---|
| **Market size** | ~500K used car transactions/year — manageable for a lean team |
| **Language** | German — same as primary expansion market (Germany) |
| **Founder base** | Founding team is Vienna-based with deep local networks |
| **Regulatory** | Straightforward GmbH incorporation, EU-standard GDPR |
| **Trust culture** | Austrians value fairness and transparency — aligns with autozon's positioning |
| **Digital readiness** | High internet penetration (93%), strong mobile adoption |
| **Competitive gap** | No dominant AI-powered P2P car platform exists in AT |

### Target Segment (Y1)

| Attribute | Profile |
|---|---|
| **Primary seller** | Private car owner, 30–55, selling a car valued €8K–35K |
| **Pain point** | Received a lowball dealer trade-in offer, knows the car is worth more |
| **Digital comfort** | Uses willhaben.at or AutoScout24 but frustrated with the process |
| **Location** | Vienna metro area (1.9M population) → expand to Graz, Linz, Salzburg |
| **Primary buyer** | Value-conscious buyer, 25–45, looking for a fair deal on a quality used car |

## Launch Phases

### Phase 1: Soft Launch (Month 1–3)

**Goal:** Validate product-market fit with 50–100 listings

| Activity | Details |
|---|---|
| **Founder-led sales** | Founding team personally onboards first 20–30 sellers from network |
| **Referral seeding** | Offer first 50 listings with waived placement fee |
| **Content creation** | 10 "Fair Value vs. Dealer Offer" comparison posts (LinkedIn, Facebook) |
| **SEO foundation** | Publish landing pages for top 20 car makes in AT (BMW, VW, Audi, Mercedes, etc.) |
| **PR outreach** | Pitch story to Austrian automotive media (Autorevue, ÖAMTC magazine) |
| **Community** | Join and contribute to Austrian car forums (motor-talk.de AT section) |

**Success criteria:**
- 50+ listings created
- 20+ completed sales
- NPS > 40 from sellers
- Average time-to-sale < 30 days

### Phase 2: Growth (Month 4–8)

**Goal:** Reach 30+ transactions/month

| Activity | Details |
|---|---|
| **Paid acquisition** | Google Ads targeting "Auto verkaufen Wien", "Auto Wert berechnen" |
| **Social proof** | Publish 10+ seller testimonials with real savings amounts |
| **willhaben.at strategy** | Target sellers who've listed for 30+ days without selling (retargeting) |
| **Partnership pilot** | Approach 3–5 independent workshops/mechanics as referral partners |
| **Content marketing** | Weekly blog: "What's my [Make] [Model] worth?" — SEO-optimized |
| **Local events** | Sponsor or attend 2–3 car meetups/events in Vienna |

**Success criteria:**
- 30+ transactions/month
- CAC < €50
- Seller conversion rate > 35%
- Brand recognition in Vienna car market

### Phase 3: Market Leadership (Month 9–12)

**Goal:** Establish autozon as the go-to fair-value car platform in Austria

| Activity | Details |
|---|---|
| **City expansion** | Targeted campaigns in Graz, Linz, Salzburg, Innsbruck |
| **Dealer onboarding** | Invite 10–20 small dealers as buyers (not sellers) — increases buyer liquidity |
| **ÖAMTC/ARBÖ partnership** | Explore co-marketing with Austrian automobile clubs (2.4M+ members combined) |
| **Financing pilot** | Launch first financing partner integration (1–2 banks/leasing companies) |
| **Media push** | Pitch "Austrian startup disrupts used car market" to Der Standard, Kurier, Presse |
| **Hire #1** | First hire: Marketing/Growth manager |

**Success criteria:**
- 100+ transactions/month
- Listings from 5+ Austrian cities
- 1+ financing partner live
- Media coverage in 2+ national outlets

## Customer Acquisition Channels

### Organic (Primary — 70% of volume target)

| Channel | Strategy | CAC |
|---|---|---|
| **SEO** | Long-tail: "BMW X3 2021 Wert", "Auto fair verkaufen Österreich" | ~€5 |
| **LinkedIn** | Founder thought leadership on fair car pricing, industry posts | €0 |
| **Referrals** | €25 credit for each referred seller who completes a sale | ~€25 |
| **Word of mouth** | Every seller who saves €3K+ becomes a natural advocate | €0 |
| **Content/Blog** | Weekly "What's it worth?" articles targeting specific models | ~€10 |

### Paid (Secondary — 30% of volume target)

| Channel | Strategy | Est. CAC |
|---|---|---|
| **Google Ads** | Search: "Auto verkaufen", "Gebrauchtwagen Bewertung" | €30–50 |
| **Facebook/Instagram** | Carousel ads showing dealer offer vs. autozon fair value | €25–40 |
| **Retargeting** | Users who started but didn't complete car listing | €15–25 |

### Blended CAC Target: **< €40/seller**

## Positioning & Messaging

### Core Message

> **"Your car is worth more than a dealer will ever tell you."**

### Message Framework

| Audience | Primary Message | Proof Point |
|---|---|---|
| **Frustrated seller** | "Stop losing thousands to dealer lowball offers" | BMW X3 example: €28,500 dealer vs. €35,000 autozon |
| **First-time seller** | "Sell your car at fair value — AI does the hard work" | 5-step wizard, AI damage scan, instant valuation |
| **Cautious buyer** | "Every car is AI-inspected and fairly priced" | Transparent condition scoring, no hidden fees |
| **Value-driven buyer** | "No dealer markup — buy directly from verified sellers" | P2P model, structured negotiation |

### Brand Voice

| Attribute | Expression |
|---|---|
| **Trustworthy** | Data-backed claims, real savings examples, no hype |
| **Direct** | Short, clear copy — no jargon, no corporate speak |
| **Empowering** | "You deserve fair value" — seller-first language |
| **Modern** | Clean design, AI-forward, tech-savvy but approachable |

## Risks & Contingencies

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Contingency |
|---|---|---|---|
| Slow initial adoption | Medium | Low revenue Y1 | Extend free placement period, increase founder-led outreach |
| High CAC in paid channels | Medium | Margin pressure | Shift budget to SEO/content, double down on referrals |
| willhaben.at launches fair-value feature | Low | Competitive threat | Deepen AI moat (damage detection, concierge), emphasize trust |
| Regulatory friction (vehicle trade laws) | Low | Compliance costs | Pre-cleared legal framework, local counsel |
| Negative early reviews | Low | Brand damage | Over-invest in support for first 100 sellers, personal concierge |
