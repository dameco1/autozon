## Patent Considerations

### Current Position

Autozon does **not** currently hold or plan to file patents. This is a deliberate strategic choice:

| Factor | Assessment |
|---|---|
| **Software patents in EU** | Generally not granted for software per se (EPC Art. 52) |
| **Cost** | €15K–€30K per patent application (EU) |
| **Time to grant** | 3–5 years |
| **Enforcement** | Expensive and uncertain for startups |
| **Alternative protection** | Trade secrets provide adequate protection for algorithms |

### Future Patent Strategy (Series A+)

| Potential Patent Areas | Feasibility |
|---|---|
| AI damage detection methodology | Medium — if novel technical effect demonstrated |
| Fair value calculation system | Low — likely considered business method |
| Matching algorithm | Low — likely considered business method |

**Recommendation:** Revisit patent strategy at Series A when resources allow proper freedom-to-operate (FTO) analysis.
