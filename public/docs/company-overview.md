# Company Overview

## Mission

To create the **fair car market** — a transparent, AI-powered platform that eliminates the information asymmetry between private car sellers and professional dealers, ensuring every vehicle is sold at its true market value.

## Vision

Become the trusted standard for peer-to-peer used car transactions across Europe, replacing opaque dealer trade-ins with a data-driven, seller-first marketplace.

---

## The Problem

The European used car market is broken by design:

- **Dealers extract 15–30% through "engineered depreciation"** — systematically undervaluing trade-ins using information asymmetry
- **Private sellers lack tools** to determine fair value, detect damage accurately, or find qualified buyers
- **Buyers face trust issues** — no reliable condition data, no standardized inspections, no structured negotiation
- **The result**: billions of euros in value transferred annually from private sellers to dealer margins

### Real-World Example

A 2021 BMW X3 xDrive20d with 45,000 km:
| Channel | Offered Price | Seller Loss |
|---|---|---|
| Dealer Trade-In | €28,500 | –€6,500 (–19%) |
| **Autozon Fair Value** | **€35,000** | **€0** |

---

## The Solution

**Autozon** is an AI-powered marketplace that levels the playing field:

1. **AI Fair Value Engine** — Calculates the true market value using real-time data (comparable listings, condition scoring, demand signals)
2. **AI Damage Detection** — Computer vision identifies and quantifies vehicle damage from photos, removing guesswork
3. **Smart Matching** — Algorithmic buyer-seller matching based on preferences, budget, and vehicle attributes
4. **Structured Negotiation** — Fair, round-based negotiation with transparent pricing anchored to fair value
5. **Acquisition Options** — Integrated financing, leasing, and insurance from vetted partners
6. **AI Concierge** — Bilingual (EN/DE) AI assistant guiding users through every step

## Business Model

### Asymmetric Marketplace Pricing

| Side | Pricing | Rationale |
|---|---|---|
| **Buyers** | Free | Maximize liquidity — every buyer makes the platform more valuable for sellers |
| **Sellers** | 2.5% success fee (capped at €499) | Aligned incentives — autozon earns only when the seller earns |
| **Placement** | €49 per listing (optional) | Commitment signal + premium matching visibility |
