# Financial Projections — 3-Year Outlook

## Assumptions & Methodology

All projections are based on:
- **Launch market**: Austria (Y1), expanding to Germany (Y2), DACH + CEE entry (Y3)
- **Average Sale Price (ASP)**: €15,000 (AT), €16,000 (DE), €12,000 (CEE blend)
- **Success fee**: 2.5% of sale price, capped at €499
- **Placement fee**: €49 per listing (credited toward success fee on completion)
- **Seller conversion rate**: 40% of listings result in a completed sale (conservative)
- **Placement uptake**: 60% of sellers opt for paid placement
- **Currency**: EUR

## Funding Scenarios

### Scenario A: Bootstrapped (No External Funding)

- Viable in base case — cash flow positive from Y1 Q2
- Slower Y2 expansion, limited marketing budget
- Team growth capped at 3–4 in Y2
- Lower risk, full founder equity retained

### Scenario B: Seed Round (€150K–300K)

- Accelerates German market entry by 3–6 months
- Enables €100K+ marketing spend in Y2
- Funds 2–3 additional hires (engineering + sales)
- Targets 2x base case Y2 revenue
- Expected dilution: 10–15%

### Scenario C: Pre-Series A (€500K–1M)

- Full DACH launch by end of Y2 (vs. Y3 in base case)
- Dedicated engineering team (5+ hires)
- Aggressive marketing: €300K+ in Y2
- Financing partner integrations accelerated
- Targets optimistic case Y2 + early Y3 numbers
- Expected dilution: 15–25%

## Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|
| Lower than expected conversion rate | Revenue shortfall | Improve matching algorithm, add AI concierge nudges |
| Higher CAC in Germany | Margin compression | Leverage organic SEO, referral programs, dealer partnerships |
| Regulatory changes (EU vehicle trade) | Compliance costs | Modular legal framework, local counsel in each market |
| Competitor entry (AutoScout24, Cazoo pivot) | Market share pressure | First-mover in fair-value P2P, deepen AI moat |
| ASP decline (economic downturn) | Lower per-txn revenue | Volume offsets: more transactions at lower prices |
| Delayed financing partner integration | Missing V2 revenue | Prioritize direct P2P value; referrals are additive, not core |
| AWS migration delays or overruns | Extended Lovable Cloud dependency | Lovable Cloud is stable for 0–5K users; migration is enhancement, not emergency |
| Higher-than-projected AWS costs | Margin pressure at scale | Reserved instances, Savings Plans, right-sizing; AWS costs offset by improved reliability and investor confidence |
