## Advisory Board (Beirat)

### Strategy

The advisory board will be **informal and equity-compensated** (0.25–0.5% per advisor, 2-year vest). Advisors provide strategic guidance without statutory authority (Beirat, not Aufsichtsrat).

### Target Advisor Profiles

| Seat | Focus Area | Ideal Background | Commitment | Status |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Automotive Industry** | Market strategy, dealer relationships, regulatory | Senior executive from AutoScout24, mobile.de, or major dealer group | 4–6 hours/month | 🔍 Recruiting |
| **Marketplace / Fintech** | Platform scaling, unit economics, marketplace dynamics | Founder/exec from successful EU marketplace (Willhaben, Shpock, etc.) | 4–6 hours/month | 🔍 Recruiting |
| **Legal / Regulatory** | Austrian corporate law, GDPR, automotive regulations | Austrian Rechtsanwalt with startup and/or automotive experience | 2–4 hours/month | 🔍 Recruiting |
| **AI / Technology** | AI strategy, computer vision, scalable ML infrastructure | CTO/VP Eng from AI-first company | 2–4 hours/month | 🔍 Recruiting |

### Advisor Compensation

| Component | Terms |
|---|---|
| **Equity** | 0.25–0.5% per advisor (virtual shares / VSOP) |
| **Vesting** | 2-year monthly vest, no cliff |
| **Cash** | None (equity-only at this stage) |
| **Total advisor pool** | 1–2% (from ESOP allocation) |

### Advisor Engagement Model

| Activity | Frequency |
|---|---|
| 1:1 call with CEO | Monthly |
| Board update email | Monthly |
| Strategic review session | Quarterly |
| Introductions & networking | Ongoing |
| Ad-hoc consultation | As needed |

## Talent Strategy

### Hiring Principles

| Principle | Rationale |
|---|---|
| **Hire slow, fire fast** | Quality over speed — every early hire shapes culture |
| **Equity-heavy compensation** | Preserve cash; attract believers in the mission |
| **Vienna-first, remote-flexible** | Build core team locally; allow DACH remote for specialists |
| **Full-stack mindset** | Early hires must wear multiple hats |
| **Cultural fit > credentials** | Startup intensity, ownership mentality, customer obsession |

### Compensation Philosophy

| Stage | Cash : Equity Ratio | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Pre-seed** | 60:40 | Below-market salary + meaningful equity |
| **Seed** | 70:30 | Competitive salary + equity |
| **Series A** | 80:20 | Market-rate salary + equity top-up |

### ESOP (Employee Stock Option Plan)

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| **Total pool** | 3.5–5% of fully diluted shares |
| **Instrument** | Virtual shares (Phantom Stock / VSOP) |
| **Standard vesting** | 4 years, 1-year cliff, monthly thereafter |
| **Acceleration** | Double-trigger on acquisition |
| **Exercise window** | 90 days post-departure |

*See [Cap Table](cap-table.md) for detailed ESOP allocation and dilution scenarios.*
